## Transitioning from Python to SQL: A Key Skill in Machine Learning and Data Science

Having covered the fundamentals of Python over the past few weeks, we are now ready to move on to another essential skill in the field of machine learning and data science: SQL (Structured Query Language). Depending on the project, you may find yourself using SQL more or less frequently. However, regardless of the extent of its use, SQL is an important skill to have on your resume.

Companies often store their data in SQL databases. As a data scientist or machine learning engineer, you will need to extract information from these databases to train your machine learning models. That's where SQL comes into play.

Over the next few weeks, we will be exploring the fundamentals of SQL, but with a unique twist. As always, be sure to follow me on social media for updates and more. See you soon!
